# Founding Vision Research Campaign

*Planning document · owned by j-queen (scope) → executed by `uni-research-sm` (campaign)*

## Purpose

Jurati has rich process machinery but **no captured vision** — no `PRODUCT-VISION.md`, no
goals, no capability map. This campaign is the founding research that lets the human and
j-queen synthesize that vision. It is scoped to *set the vision*, not to settle
everything.

**North star this research serves:** *a world-class platform for the human's own
development and delivery — a deterministic harness that drives structured SDLC and
research workflows, with Unimatrix as its co-evolving memory/context engine, controlled
and increasingly autonomous. Personal/OSS-first, architected to extend to enterprise
without a rewrite. Jurati as the queen over the ecosystem.*
